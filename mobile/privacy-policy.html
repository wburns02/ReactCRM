Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Mac Septic ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Mac Septic Field mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you log in to the App, we collect your email address and name as provided by your employer's CRM system. We do not create user accounts — your credentials are managed by your organization's administrator.

Location Data

The App collects your device's GPS location when you start or complete a work order. This is used to verify arrival at job sites and is stored with the work order record. Location is only collected when you actively use the App's work order features, not in the background.

Photos

The App allows you to take photos of work sites for documentation purposes (before/after photos, manifests). Photos are uploaded to our secure servers and associated with specific work orders. We do not access your photo library beyond what you explicitly select.

Device Information

We collect basic device information (device type, operating system version) for app diagnostics and to ensure compatibility.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and provide access to your assigned work orders
  • To verify your arrival at job sites
  • To store work order documentation (photos, signatures, notes)
  • To sync data between your device and our servers
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Your data is shared only with:

  • Your Employer: Work order data, including photos, location, and signatures, is accessible to authorized personnel at your organization.
  • Service Providers: We use Railway for hosting and Expo for app distribution. These providers process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers hosted by Railway. Authentication tokens are stored using your device's secure storage (iOS Keychain). We use HTTPS for all data transmission. Offline data is stored locally on your device and synced when connectivity is restored.

5. Data Retention

Work order data is retained according to your organization's data retention policies. When you log out, locally cached data is cleared from your device.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting your organization's administrator or by emailing us at privacy@macseptic.com.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through the App or via email.
