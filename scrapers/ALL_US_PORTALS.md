# National Septic Permit Portal Catalog

> **Generated:** 2026-01-18
> **Purpose:** Comprehensive catalog of all public septic permit repositories in the United States
> **Status:** Phase 1 Complete - Deep National Research

## Executive Summary

This catalog documents **50 states** worth of septic permit portal research, identifying:
- **38+ Statewide/Regional Online Portals** with direct search capabilities
- **200+ County-level portals** across various platforms
- **Key platforms identified:** Accela, EnerGov, MyGovernmentOnline, Ascent, OpenGov, custom state systems
